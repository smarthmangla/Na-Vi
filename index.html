About Us

Our story started 2 years ago with humble beginnings and a vision to make the world a better place to live in. We are constantly and relentlessly striving forward to develop and innovate new practices and principles in renewable energy, rain water harvesting, vermicompost, reducing food wastage. Driven by strong values and a mission to help everyone access smarter, cleaner energy, the company continues to innovate and deliver on the shared value of Kaitiakitanga - the Maori principle of guardianship. It’s a constant reminder we have a duty of care to look after our place, our people and the generations to follow.
